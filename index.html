How It Works

AI-assisted email responses with human oversight

Lead Monitoring & Capture

Monitors your database for new form submissions. Automatically stores lead data in organized databases for tracking.

AI Email Generation

AI analyzes lead details and searches your project database containing past project names, customers, locations, and highlights to generate personalized email responses with relevant examples and professional tone. This creates more human-like, contextual responses.

Human Approval & Send

Generated emails are sent to you for approval. You can approve as-is or request revisions. Human approval is optional and can be enabled or disabled. Only approved emails are sent to leads.
